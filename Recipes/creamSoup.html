French cream soup

The deep earthy flavours of a classic home-made cream of mushroom soup recipe can’t be beat, especially when it’s ready in minutes. You’ll never open a can of mushroom soup after trying our recipe!

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Champignons
  • Water
  1. Boil 2 l. of water and add peeled, cut potatoes
  2. Add chopped garlic and onion
  3. Grate cheese and cut mushrooms, add to the soup
  4. Add 1 tbs. of flour and boil for 10 mins
